About Nette Framework
*********************

.[perex]
Real programmers don't use frameworks. They write web applications through command-line straight to the server. We salute them. For the rest of us there are framworks. Thanks to Nette Framework you will write less, have cleaner code and your work will bring you joy. Nette Framework comes with a whole array of **technical innovations** - you will create perfectly secured applications, which will be easily extendable. It has an active [community of  PHP developers | community] and many major companies have put it to good use.
